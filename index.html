IUCN Visualizer Release

A Java visualizer based on JGraphX framework, under BSD license. The project is being developed for the International Union for Conservation of Nature, to provide a tool for scientists to visualize their conceptual models of biological systems.

For descriptions and instructions of the project, please refer to project Wiki.

To download newest version of the app, please see Releases.
